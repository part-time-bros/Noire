Our Story

From a single
chair to a
movement.

NOIRE began in 2019 in a modest 600-square-foot space in Bandra West, Mumbai. Founder and Creative Director Priya Menon had one conviction: that the quality of grooming services available in India's premium urban market didn't match the quality of the people seeking them.

Indian salon-goers were well-travelled. They'd experienced Neville Daniel in London, Frédéric Fekkai in New York. They came back to Mumbai and found the gap jarring — not in the skill of Indian artists, who were exceptional, but in the experience itself. The consultation was rushed. The environment felt clinical or, at the other extreme, garish. The craft wasn't communicated.

"Indian clients didn't need to fly abroad for world-class hair. They needed someone to build it here."

In the first year, NOIRE operated with three stylists and a waiting list. By 2021, the studio expanded into its current 2,400-square-foot home — a space designed not to look like a salon, but to feel like a private atelier. No neon. No clutter. Deliberate light. Considered materials.

Today, a team of 18 artists serves a client base that spans Bollywood stylists, architects, entrepreneurs, and university students. The price point is premium. The welcome is the same for everyone.

NOIRE is not a chain. It has no ambitions to become one. We believe the moment you scale a luxury experience past its breaking point, you've lost the thing people came for.

Our Journey

Seven years
in the making.

2019
NOIRE Opens

Priya Menon opens the first NOIRE studio in a 600 sq ft space in Bandra West. Three stylists. A waiting list in the first month.

2020
Skincare Wing Added

Sneha Rao joins as Skin Lead, expanding NOIRE into facials and wellness treatments. The skincare programme becomes the studio's fastest-growing service.

2021
Studio Expansion

NOIRE moves into its current 2,400 sq ft home — a purpose-designed atelier with dedicated colouring stations, a wellness room, and a nail studio.

2022
Bridal Programme Launch

The NOIRE bridal programme launches with Kavya Sharma leading. Within six months, the bridal calendar is booked out three seasons in advance.

2023
Men's Grooming Studio

A dedicated men's grooming suite opens within the studio — a first in Bandra West. Rohan Das joins as Grooming Specialist.

2025
12,000 Clients Served

A quiet milestone. No press release. Just a number that means 12,000 people left the studio feeling like themselves — or a better version of it.

The Space

A space designed
for attention.

The NOIRE studio was designed by Mumbai-based architecture firm Studio Anagram with a single brief: remove everything that isn't necessary, and make the rest feel considered.

Warm concrete, aged brass fixtures, and matte black tools. Lighting calibrated for accurate colour assessment. Each station designed for the artist's ergonomics, not just client comfort. The environment is itself a signal that what happens here is taken seriously.
